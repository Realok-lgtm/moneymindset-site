Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

Money Mindset ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Money Mindset mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Money Mindset:

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and password when you create an account.
  • Profile Data: Avatar photo (if you choose to upload one).
  • Usage Data: Lesson completion data, quiz scores, XP and MindCoin balances, achievement progress, streak data, simulated investment portfolio data, and general app interaction data.
  • Purchase History: Records of in-app purchases and subscription status (managed by Apple and RevenueCat).
  • Diagnostic Data: Crash logs and performance data to help us improve the app (collected via Sentry).

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Money Mindset app experience.
  • Track your learning progress, achievements, and streaks.
  • Display leaderboard rankings.
  • Send push notifications (daily reminders, streak alerts) if you opt in.
  • Process in-app purchases and manage subscriptions.
  • Diagnose and fix crashes and performance issues.

Third-Party Services

We share data with the following third-party services that help us operate the app:

  • Supabase: Cloud database and authentication provider. Stores your account data, progress, and preferences.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and in-app purchase management. Receives Apple ID purchase data to verify your subscription status.
  • Apple: Processes in-app purchases and Sign in with Apple authentication.
  • Sentry: Crash reporting and performance monitoring. Receives anonymized error data. Screenshots are never captured.
  • DiceBear API: Avatar generation service. Receives avatar configuration parameters only.

Data Retention

Your data is stored for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our servers and local storage.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app by going to Settings and selecting "Delete Account." This action is irreversible and removes all your data from our database, authentication records, and local device storage.

Data Export

You can export a copy of your data in JSON format from within the app by going to Settings and selecting "Export Data."

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data transmission. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Money Mindset is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete that information.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Export your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing.
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

California (CCPA): We do not sell personal information to third parties.

Australia (Privacy Act 1988): You may access, correct, or delete your personal data by contacting us or using the in-app tools.

Tracking

Money Mindset does not track you across other apps or websites. We do not use advertising identifiers (IDFA) or share data with advertising networks.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
