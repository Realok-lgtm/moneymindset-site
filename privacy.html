Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 11, 2026

Money Mindset ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Money Mindset iOS mobile application (the "App"), a gamified financial psychology education app developed by Reuben Schultz, an individual based in Australia. The App is available on the Apple App Store with Bundle ID com.wealthmind.wealthmind. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use the App. It applies to all users of the App, with specific rights noted for residents of the United States (including California under the CCPA), Australia, the European Union (GDPR), and other applicable jurisdictions.

By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide, improve, and secure the App. We do not collect location data, health or fitness data, real financial information, browsing history, or any data from other apps or websites. We do not use the Advertising Identifier (IDFA) or engage in cross-app or cross-website tracking.

Information You Provide:

  • Email address, display name (chosen at signup), and password (hashed by our backend provider).
  • Optional profile avatar photo (uploaded via camera or photo library and stored securely).
  • Gameplay and progress data: lesson completion status, quiz scores, XP points, MindCoin balances, achievement progress, daily streak count, and simulated investment portfolio holdings/transactions (all fictional; no real money is involved).
  • Subscription and purchase history (managed through Apple and our third-party provider).
  • App interaction data: features used, lessons viewed, and feed cards seen (used only to enforce freemium limits and improve user experience).

Automatically Collected Information:

  • Anonymized crash logs and performance metrics (via Sentry; screenshots are explicitly disabled).
  • Technical data necessary for App functionality (e.g., device type and iOS version for compatibility).

Authentication Options:

  • Email + password, or Sign in with Apple.

We do not collect data from users under the age of 13. The App is not directed at children and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Deliver the gamified educational experience, including lesson access, quizzes, simulations, streaks, achievements, and MindCoin rewards.
  • Enforce freemium usage limits (e.g., 1 lesson/day, 1 simulation/day, 1 quiz/day, 10 feed cards/day for free users).
  • Process and manage subscriptions and in-app purchases (Premium Monthly at $6.99/month, Premium Annual at $39.99/year, and MindCoin consumable packs).
  • Send optional push notifications (daily reminders, streak alerts, weekly paycheck reminders) only after you explicitly grant permission via the iOS system prompt.
  • Analyze App performance, fix bugs, and improve features (using anonymized data only).
  • Respond to your support requests and fulfill legal obligations.

All portfolio data is purely simulated and fictional; we never handle real money, investments, or financial accounts.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to any third parties (as defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act). We share data only as strictly necessary and with appropriate safeguards:

  • With Service Providers: We engage trusted third-party services under data-processing agreements that require them to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose information if required by law, to protect our rights, or in response to valid legal processes.
  • Business Transfers: In the unlikely event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred, with notice provided where required.

We do not share data with advertisers or for marketing purposes beyond the App's own functionality.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services, each with a clearly defined purpose and limited data access:

  1. Supabase (supabase.co) -- Provides backend database, authentication, and file storage for all user accounts, gameplay data, avatars, and purchases. All data is hosted in the Sydney, Australia region. Row Level Security (RLS) is enabled on every table to ensure users can only access their own data.
  2. RevenueCat (revenuecat.com) -- Manages all in-app subscriptions and purchases. It receives purchase data from Apple to verify entitlements and subscription status. No personal data beyond what is required for purchase verification is shared.
  3. Apple -- Processes all payments through the App Store and handles Sign in with Apple authentication. Apple receives only the information required for these services.
  4. Sentry (sentry.io) -- Provides crash reporting and performance monitoring. Only anonymized error data is sent; screenshots are disabled. Trace sampling rate is limited to 20% in production.
  5. DiceBear API (dicebear.com) -- Generates default avatar illustrations when requested. It receives only avatar style configuration parameters and no personal information or user identifiers.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable and appropriate technical and organizational security measures, including encryption in transit and at rest (via Supabase), access controls, and regular security reviews. Supabase's Row Level Security and our backend architecture ensure data is isolated per user. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide the App's services, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes. When you delete your account, we permanently delete your data from all Supabase tables, authentication records, and local device storage within a reasonable timeframe (typically within 30 days). Anonymized crash logs are retained by Sentry only as long as needed for debugging (usually 90 days or less). Aggregated, non-identifiable usage statistics may be retained indefinitely for product improvement.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights, which you can exercise directly in the App:

  • Account Deletion: Go to Settings > Delete Account. This irreversibly removes your data from all Supabase database tables, authentication records, RevenueCat records (where applicable), and local device storage.
  • Data Export: Go to Settings > Export Data to receive a JSON file of your personal data via the iOS share sheet.
  • Access and Correction: You may view and update your profile information (display name, avatar, email) within the App at any time.
  • Push Notifications: You may opt out at any time via iOS Settings > Notifications > Money Mindset.
  • Objection / Withdrawal of Consent: Where applicable, you may object to certain processing or withdraw consent (subject to legal limitations).

We respond to valid requests without undue delay and, where required by law, free of charge.
